About Us

Zaitoon is a perfect place in town to taste the best Arabic Food and BBQ, established as one of the most popular entrant into the string which serves Middle-East food along with Indian/Chinese. Authentic Arabian and Indian cuisines made with passion and attention to detail treat your taste buds well.

Being passionate about cooking, Fardan T.K and Naufal Abdulla laid their first step towards making their dream come true – initiating “Zaitoon” on October 11th 2009 at Adyar with a vision of providing best quality food service. The primary challenges of being a multi-cuisine restaurant are to maintain the authenticity, food innovation, hospitality and to incorporate new ideas in restaurant business in every aspect. Very keen in maintaining the food quality and taste, we also desire Zaitoon to be preferred for its refreshing ambience to dine in even for an impromptu lunch or dinner.

With a high degree of commitment and quality, our restaurant chain has expanded gloriously with 5 branches in Chennai and 1 in Bangalore. Without compromising on quality and service, we take every step to make your dining experience, a pleasurable one.
